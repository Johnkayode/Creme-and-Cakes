About Creme n' Cakes

Rebranding the cake culture..

The Creme n' Cakes Bakeries started off in 2018 by Mrs. Omoni Craig with its main branch in Allen Avenue, Ikeja. Over the years, Creme n' Cakes has grown larger with over 10 branches in Lagos and Abuja.
Our aim at Creme n' Cakes is to deliver the best of cake delicacies to your footstep.

Features

Fast Delivery

Maximum of 4 hours after taking orders, Creme n' Cakes deliver your package to your footstep.

24/7 Customer Service

At Creme n' Cakes, we are concerned about the way our customers are treated and we attend to all complaints quickly.

Quality and Affordable

Creme n' Cakes deliver quality food and at the same time, we make sure they are pocket-friendly.

Free Delivery Services

Ikeja, Lagos and Gwagalada, Abuja environs enjoy free delivery services while other areas are required to pay a little fee depending on the area.

Discount

At Creme n' Cakes, we honour our customers' birthdays and offer them a 20% discount on birthday cakes.

No Refunds

However, Creme n' Cakes do not give refunds to customers upon error in any purchase made.
